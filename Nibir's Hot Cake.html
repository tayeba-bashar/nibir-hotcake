Nibir's Hot Cake

The recepie for this dish is semi-complex and takes a long while to prepare, but it is very worth it. I love this dish because it is something my grandma used to make, and it is like Sumi's Hot Cake, everybody likes them. (pssst, its better but don't let anybody hear that)

Ingredients:

  1. 3 cup all purpose flour
  2. 2 beaten egg
  3. 2 teaspoon baking soda
  4. 2 teaspoon vanilla essence
  5. 2 cup powdered sugar
  6. 1 cup butter
  7. 2 cup milk

Preparation:

  1. Step 01: Choose a Recipe
  2. Step 02: Choose the Right Baking Pans
  3. Step 03: Allow Ingredients to Reach Room Temperature
  4. Step 04: Prep the Pans
  5. Step 05: Preheat the Oven
  6. Step 06: Stir Together Dry Ingredients
  7. Step 07: Combine the Butter and Sugar
  8. Step 08: Add Eggs One at a Time
  9. Step 09: Alternate Adding Dry and Wet Ingredients
  10. Step 10: Pour Batter Into Pans and Bake
  11. Step 11: Check Cake for Doneness
  12. Step 12: Cool the Cake
  13. Step 13: Assemble the Cake
  14. Step 14: Add the First Coat of Frosting
  15. Step 15: Frost and Decorate

The mixure of flavors is AMAZING. Bland of sourness relaxes the greasy taste of sweet and the way of preparation makes it feel light almost as dietary, but do not be fooled this dish is very calorie dense.
